Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. It allows subscribers to watch content without commercials, with new titles added regularly.

Netflix offers plans ranging from ₹149 to ₹649 per month, with options including Mobile, Basic, Standard, and Premium plans, each providing different levels of streaming quality and number of simultaneous screens.

On Netflix, you can watch a wide array of content including popular TV shows, movies, documentaries, anime, and original series across various genres. The platform frequently updates its library with new titles and exclusive releases.

You can watch Netflix on a variety of devices, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, laptops, and streaming devices like Roku, Apple TV, and Amazon Fire Stick. Additionally, you can stream Netflix through gaming consoles such as PlayStation and Xbox.
